Problem summary:

The farmers who use these types conventional backpack sprayer faces many types of problems like fatigue, tiredness, pain in spiral cord and muscles etc. Following problems can take place by use of this conventional type of pump.

Common Problems:

  1. Heavy in weight causes difficulty in lifting manually.
  2. Fatigue to the operator due to heavy weight.
  3. Due to heavy weight during spraying, operator feel very tiredness and fatigue which reduces his efficiency.
  4. Big size of pump cause inconvenience to the operator.
  5. Poor selection and quality of equipment.
  6. These problems combined with a lack of awareness and technical knowledge and inadequate maintenance and poor field use of equipment has led to unacceptable risks to environment and human health.
